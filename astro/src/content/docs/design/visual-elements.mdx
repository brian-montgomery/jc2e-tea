The current visual elements that are used in the various prototypes. As this is a simple homemade print-and-play scenario, we are limited to Free Use and Creative Commons based resources.

## Fonts

These are similar to the fonts used in the base game, but are not direct matches.

* [Baskervville](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Baskervville) at Google Fonts

* [Cormorant](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cormorant) at Google Fonts
* [Great Vibes](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Great+Vibes) at Google Fonts

* Parliament Cards: [Brim Narrow](https://www.myfonts.com/collections/brim-narrow-font-jamie-clarke-type/)
