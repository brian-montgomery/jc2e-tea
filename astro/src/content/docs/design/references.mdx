The following is a list of materials read or reviewed during the creation of this scenario. There was a good bit of focus on understanding the economics of Colonial trade, British tax policy and the motivations for the Boston Tea Party and the American Revolution, in addition to researching the East India Company.

## Books

* Barck, Oscar Theodore Jr. & Lefler, Hugh Talmage (1968 - 2nd Edition). [Colonial America](https://archive.org/details/colonialamericabarcklefler). New York: MacMillian Company.
* Beckert, Sven (2014). [Empire of Cotton](https://archive.org/details/empireofcottongl0000beck). United States: Alfred A. Knopf, Random House. 
* Bunker, Nick (2014). [An Empire on the Edge](https://archive.org/details/empireonedgehowb0000nick). New York: Alfred A. Knopf, Random House. 
* Chaudhuri, K. N. (2006). [The Trading World of Asia and the English East India Company: 1660-1760](https://www.google.com/books/edition/The_Trading_World_of_Asia_and_the_Englis/9xt7Fgzq9e8C?hl=en). United Kingdom: Cambridge University Press.
* Finn, Margot (2018). [The East India Company at home, 1757-1857](https://archive.org/details/dli.doa.006/). London: UCL Press.
* Irwin, D. A. (2017). [Clashing Over Commerce: A History of US Trade Policy](https://www.google.com/books/edition/Clashing_Over_Commerce/GEY_DwAAQBAJ?hl=en). United Kingdom: University of Chicago Press.
* Keay, John (1991). [The Honourable Company: a History of the English East India Company](https://archive.org/details/honourablecompan0000keay/). London: HarperCollins.
* Morris, Margaret Shove (1914). [Colonial Trade of Maryland, 1689-1715](http://catalog.hathitrust.org/Record/001122951). Dissertation for Byrn Mawr College.
* Patton, R. H. (2009). [Patriot Pirates: The Privateer War for Freedom and Fortune in the American Revolution](https://www.google.com/books/edition/_/8bg_m-APQ5MC?hl=en). United States: Knopf Doubleday Publishing Group.
* Randall, W. S. (2022). [The Founders' Fortunes: How Money Shaped the Birth of America](https://www.google.com/books/edition/The_Founders_Fortunes/TKxPEAAAQBAJ?hl=en). United States: Penguin Publishing Group.
