This is currently __version {process.env.npm_package_version}__ of this design. For this version,
the [events](/jc2e-tea/components/decks/events/) and [smuggler cards](/jc2e-tea/components/decks/smuggler/)
have been reworked and the [setup cards](/jc2e-tea/components/decks/setup/) have been introduced.
Feedback on how those play, as well as any balance issues, is appreciated.

## Main Design Issues

In version 0.5.x, the focus is on the following areas:

<div class="bold-list">
1. [__Setup Cards__](/jc2e-tea/components/decks/setup/).
   If any of the setup cards feels significantly under- or overpowered, please provide feedback on that.
2. [__Smuggler Cards__](/jc2e-tea/components/decks/smuggler/).
   Currently, the orders on the smuggler cards are relatively evenly distributed, but somewhat random. We're trying to determine how that feels versus making more "trade pathing" like the players are required to do. Also, the distribution of the trade orders is leaning slightly toward the (higher valued) starting colonized regions, that distribution may need to lean heavier.
3. [__Event Cards__](/jc2e-tea/components/decks/events/).
   -  _{frontmatter._["event.movement"]}_.
      The {frontmatter._["event.movement"]} has been introduced. We're trying to see if having the {frontmatter._["event.movement"]} at the beginning of some events (instead of at the end like
      the Elephant's March) helps make things simpler to run and doesn't require a piece (although
      it probably helps). The downside of having it at the beginning is that it lowers the possibility
      space of the events and also lowers the tension between turns.
   -  _Current Balance._
      The balance of the events is still in flux. The amount of military action events have been increased and feedback on how that feels is appreciated.
4. [__Scenario End__](/jc2e-tea/rules/scenario-end/).
   One ending condition for this scenario is having no colonized regions remaining in America.
   While this feels like a logical place to end (the {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} has no
   purpose), the narrative feels somewhat awkward and the ending feels abrupt. There are currently no special endgame scoring adjustments that happen, other than the penalties for region loss.
</div>
