The cards can be downloaded as a PDF or viewed as separate pages and printed.
However, printing only works from a Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, etc).

This print-and-play is designed for the front and backs of the cards to be folded
over. The bridge-sized cards will print with the top and bottom edges aligned,
while the mini-euro sized cards should be folded on the side edges.
