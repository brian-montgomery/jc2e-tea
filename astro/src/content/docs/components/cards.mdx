### Tariffs

The tariffs were to encourage British Trade and penalize trade with other nations. 

#### Commutation Act

- Increase the Value of Tea by 2.

History: Government significantly reduced tariffs on tea.
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commutation_Act_1784
- https://www.jstor.org/stable/2598638

#### Indemnity Act (Townshend Act)

- Increase the Value of Tea by 1.
- Regions: All
- Other Names: "EIC Indemnity", "EIC Tax Relief", "Colonial Tea Act"

Goal: Increase company profits on Tea.

History: Part of the Townshend Acts, this reduced taxes on the EIC for importing Tea in England.
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Townshend_Acts#The_Indemnity_Act_1767

#### Molasses Act (Navigation Act)

- Colonial Duty
   - Leave trade closed.
   - Customs Office gets no bonus.
- Region: New England (and SC?)

**Note:** Start with this in scenario?

Goal: Minor hit as this law was mostly ignored.
 - Start with this one in play, rolling for NE?

History: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Molasses_Act

#### Sugar Act

- Colonial Duty
   - £1 per successful trade (smugglers) in the Colonies.
- Region: New England (and Mid)

Goal: Harder hit as this was slightly stronger enforced.

History: Sequel to Molasses Tax (1733).
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sugar_Act

#### Revenue Act (Townshend Act)

- Colonial Duty
   - Add £1 per region in a successful trade in the Colonies.
   - Add bonus of £1 to all manufacturing enterprises.
   - Increase the Value of Tea by 1.
- Region: All

**Note:** Really good if we make Tea Crates worth per order per region.
- Feels different and supplies a different goal.

**Note:** Lots of abilities here. Consider breaking this up into a couple (or lowering its effect).

History: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Townshend_Acts#The_Revenue_Act_1767
> "Placed taxes on glass, lead, "painters' colors" (paint), paper, and tea. It also gave the supreme court of each colony the power to issue "writs of assistance", general warrants that could be issued to customs officers and used to search private property for smuggled goods"

#### Stamp Act

- Colonial Duty
   - Take £1 per successful trade (firms and smugglers) in the Colonies.
- Region: All

Goal: 

+ CT: Add £1 per successful trade in the Colonies.
+ CT: Add bonus of £1 to all manufacturing enterprises.
- Perform a reaction roll in every Colonial region.

Note: There was a second stamp at in 1765 that also affected the EIC: "An Act for granting to His Majesty certain Duties on the Exportation of Coals, and of several East India Goods, and upon Policies of Assurance; for retaining, upon the Exportation of White Callicoes and Mustins, a further Part of the Duties paid on the Importation thereof; and for obviating a Doubt with respect to Stamp Duties imposed upon Deeds by Two former Acts." - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_acts_of_the_Parliament_of_Great_Britain_from_1765

### Enforcement

These were acts of authority and punishment on the colonies.

#### Boston Port Act (Coercive Act)

- Close the port in the NE region.
- Add 1 regiment to military.
- Region: New England

History: Was the result of the Boston Tea Party. Little different here.
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boston_Port_Act

#### Commissioners of Customs Act (Townshend Act)

- Increase the Value of Tea.
- Add to Government Admin budget.
- Increase amount of die that Customs Officer rolls?
   - Increase unrest when a 6 is rolled.
- Region: All

Goal: Demand is staying high due to crack down on illegal imports.

History: Added customs board in North American colonies
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Townshend_Acts#The_Commissioners_of_Customs_Act_1767

#### Courts for Smuggler Prosecution (Townshend Act)

- Increase the Value of Tea by 1.
- Increase Commissioners Power.
- Region: LS, Mid

Goal: Illegal tea running rampant.

History: Vice-Admiralty Court Act (1768 Townshend)
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Townshend_Acts#The_Vice_Admiralty_Court_Act_1768

#### Proclamation Line

- Reduce the cost the military in America.
   - Add regiments to military (at reduced price?)
- Add strength to Natives.
- Add unrest (particular)
- Region: All. Chesapeake and (Possible Middle or NE)

History
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Proclamation_of_1763

#### Quartering Act (Townshend Act)

- Reduce the cost the military in America.
   - Add regiments to military (at reduced price?)
- Other Names: Military Quartering Required (Townshend)
- Region: Middle (and Chesapeake)

History: Attempt to make military less expensive.
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quartering_Acts#Quartering_Act_1765
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Townshend_Acts#The_New_York_Restraining_Act_1767
