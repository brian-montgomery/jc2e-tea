This is an overview of how things differ in this scenario from the other scenarios of John Company.

## London Season

*  At the end of this phase, leave any remaining pensioners in the pensioners box.

## Family Action

*  When performing the **Enlist Writer** action, you may place a family member in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]}' treasury. Any family members here are considered collectors.
*  When performing the **Enlist Officer** action, you may place a family member in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.military.full"]}'s box.
*  At the end of this phase, when adding Company Shares, raise the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each Company Debt lowered.

## Firms

*  During investment, a firm manager may take a bank loan.
   *  Place a red cube in the firm's Shares box for each debt taken.
   *  Add £5 to the firm's treasury.
   *  Lower the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by 1 space.
*  During investment, a firm manager may close a bank loan.
   *  Pay £5 from the firm's treasury.
   *  Remove a red cube in the firm's Shares box.
   *  Raise the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by 1 space.
*  The firm strategy applies to which port in America a firm wants to initiate trade.

## Hiring

*  The additional Company and {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} roles are hired.
*  At the end of this phase, return any remaining pensioners to their respective family supply.

## China

<div class="indent">
   ### Superintendent of China
   *  If the company controls no regions with export icons,
      the Superintendent of China may trade silver instead of opium.
      Add £2 to the Company Balance, if the trade is successful.
      Treat the action as having 1 export icon for Bonuses and Tea.
   *  After the Superintendent's turn, move to the America board before proceeding on
      the ribbon.

   ### Tea
   *  Add {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}'s (or {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}'s) treasury, using the {frontmatter._["tea.track"]}.
      *  If the Superintendent of China traded silver, use 1 export icon as the base multiplier.
   *  If the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} is occupied, perform the Tea Auction.

   ### America
   *  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]} decides the order of operations.
      *  Firms will roll for trade.
      *  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]} draws a smuggler card and
         may roll for customs.
         *  The {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]} may roll for trade, if present.
      *  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]} may roll for additional military units
         or to open trade orders.
      *  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.military.full"]} may roll to deploy.
</div>

## Revenue

Revenue must be calculated for all firms, the {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} and the Company.

<div class="indent">
   ### Firms
   *  When calculating expenses, count each debt (red cube) as a share.
   *  Firms may take emergency investment or emergency loans. When taking an emergency loan:
      *  Place a red cube in the firm's Shares box for each loan taken. Do not count these as
         additional expenses for this round.
      *  Decrease the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each loan taken.
   *  When paying dividends, count each red cube as a share. Each must be paid per dividend.

   ### {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]}
   *  Calculate expenses for {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} (Military and Debt),
      paying them from its treasury.
      *  Any debt is taken as emergency funding. If more than one is needed, the
         {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]} is fired immediately.

   ### Company
   *  Add active {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the Company expenses.
   *  Lower the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each emergency loan taken.
</div>

## Events in India

*  After resolving all storms and events in India, roll for and resolve storms and events in America, using the storm die and the Events in America deck.
   *  Any storms will apply based on the port (firm strategy) chosen, just like in India.

## Parliament Meets

*  At the start of the Phase, if the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs.full"]} is vacant,
   have the Prime Minister nominate a {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs2"]} for the turn.
*  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs.full"]} (or the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs2"]})
   draws two {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]} cards, selecting one if the vote passes and
   using the other if the vote fails.

## Upkeep & Refresh

*  Move all {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the bottom half of the
   {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}'s treasury.
*  Any exhausted officers and military units in the
   {frontmatter._["role.govt.military.full"]} become active.
   Any local alliances are exhausted.
*  Return collectors to the {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]}' box.

## Final Scoring

*  Lower the firm value by 1 per red cube in its Shares box.
