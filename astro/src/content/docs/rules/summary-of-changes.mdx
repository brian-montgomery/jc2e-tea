This is an overview of how things differ in this scenario from the other scenarios of John Company.

## London Season

*  At the end of this phase, leave any pensioners in the pensioners box.

## Family Action

*  When performing the **Enlist Writer** action, you may place a family member in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]}'s box. Any family members here are considered collectors.
*  When performing the **Enlist Officer** action, you may place a family member in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.military.full"]}'s box.
*  At the end of this phase, when adding Company Shares, raise the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each Company Debt lowered.

## Firms

*  Firms may take debt during the **Firms** action.
   *  Place a red cube in the firm's Shares box for each debt taken.
   *  Decrease the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each debt taken.
*  During investment, the firm manager may decide whether to add to their London treasury or to remove a debt. If a debt is removed, increase the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each.
*  The firm strategy applies to which port in America they want to trade at.

## Hiring

*  At the end of this phase, return any remaining pensioners to their respective family supply.

## China

<div class="indent">
### Superintendent of China
*  If desired, the Superintendent of China may trade silver instead of opium.
   Gain £2 to the Company Balance, if successful. Treat the action as having 1 export icon.
*  After the Superintendent's turn, move to the America board before proceeding on
   the ribbon.

### Tea
*  Add {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}'s (or {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}'s) treasury, using the {frontmatter._["tea.track"]}.
   *  If the Superintendent of China traded silver, use 1 export icon as the base.
*  If the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} is occupied, perform the Tea Auction.

### America
*  Draw a smuggler card first.
*  Then, the {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]} decides the order.
   *  Firms and {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]} roll for trade (and customs).
   *  Perform any military action in America.
</div>

## Revenue

<div class="indent">
### Company
*  Add active {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the Company expenses.
*  Decrease the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each emergency loan taken.

### Firms
*  Firms may take emergency investment or emergency loans. When taking an emergency loan:
   *  Place a red cube in the firm's Shares box for each debt taken.
   *  Decrease the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each debt taken.
*  When paying dividends, count each red cube as a share. Each must be paid per dividend.
   *  Red cubes require at least one dividend. If unable to pay one, a firm must take
      an emergency loan.

### {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]}
*  Calculate expenses for {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} (Military and Debt), paying them from its treasury.
   *  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor"]} may take the first debt as normal, not as an emergency loan.
   *  Any additional debt are taken as emergency loans.
</div>

## Events in India

*  After resolving all events and storms in India, perform the same in America.

## Parliament Meets

*  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs.full"]} draws two {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]}
   cards, selecting one if the law passes and using the other if the law fails.
   *  If the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs.full"]} is vacant, have the Prime Minister nominate
      a First Lord of Trade to select an.

## Upkeep & Refresh

*  Return collectors to the {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs"]}'s box.
*  Move any remaining active {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the lower section of the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}'s treasury.

## Final Scoring

*  Lower the **Firm value** by 1 per red cube in its Shares box.
