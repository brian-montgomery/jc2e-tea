_{frontmatter._["game.title.full"]}_ is very similar to other scenarios in John Company.
Each game turn consists of phases performed in the following order:

<div id="phases" class="roman">
1.  **The London Season.**
   Officeholders retire and compete for prestige
cards.
2. **Family.**
   Players invest in the Company, Firms and increase their holdings.
   Then new Company shares may be created.
-  **Firms.**
   Firm receive investments and determine their strategy for trade in America.
3. **Hiring.**
   The Company and {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} fill all vacant positions.
4. **Company and Department Operation.**
   Each Company office operates following the red ribbon from left to right on the game board.
   When China is reached, follow the red ribbon on the scenario board from right to left.
5. **Bonuses.**
   Players take bonus income.
6. **Revenue.**
   The Company, {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} and Firms pays their expenses, the Chairman and Firms may pay
   dividends, and the Company adjusts its Standing.
7. **Events in India and America.**
   Storms and events in India are resolved, followed by storms and events in America.
8. **Parliament Meets.**
   Parliament meets to vote on a law, policy and act from the {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]}.
9. **Upkeep and Refresh.**
   Players pay upkeep on their prizes and prepare for the next game turn.
</div>
