In this phase, all vacant offices in the Company and the {frontmatter._['role.govt.office']} are filled through hiring.

## Company Offices

These are the additional company officeholders available.

### {frontmatter._['role.company.tea']}

The Chairman hires from any current officeholder except the Chairman, Director of Trade or a Governor. If all offices are vacant, any writer can be chosen. _Note: Commanders are not officeholders._

### {frontmatter._['role.company.tea2']}

The <Role key="role.company.tea2"/> is hired (like the <Role key="role.company.tea"/>) by the Chairman and is subject to attrition like any other office. _Note: Available when Tea Regulation is passed._

## {frontmatter._['role.govt.office']}

Instead of following the nepotism rule, some {frontmatter._['role.govt.office']} offices use the following:

*  __Pensioners__:  When hiring for the {frontmatter._['role.govt.office']}, a pensioner who did not retire this turn may be hired. _Retirees and pensioners from previous turns are never considered._
* __Preference for Trophies__: When hiring for the {frontmatter._['role.govt.office']}, certain positions have a preference for trophies. When they do, you must either select a candidate from a family with one or more trophies or get their consent to choose another player.
* __Preference for Passed Laws__: When hiring for the {frontmatter._['role.govt.office']}, certain positions have a preference for passed laws. When they do, you must either select a candidate from a family with one or more passed law tokens or get their consent to choose another player.

Each office is hired in a different manner described below.

### {frontmatter._['role.govt.governor.full']}

The Prime Minister chooses from the {frontmatter._['role.govt.military.full']} or the {frontmatter._['role.govt.customs.full']}. If both are vacant, then choose from any remaining pensioners, with a preference for trophies or passed laws.

### {frontmatter._['role.govt.customs.full']}

The <Role key="role.govt.governor.full"/> hires from the remaining pensioners or the tax collectors. If still none are available, the {frontmatter._['role.govt.governor.full']} assumes the role.

### {frontmatter._['role.govt.military.full']}

The <Role key="role.govt.governor.full"/> hires from any officers or pensioners with trophies. If none are available, choose from any piece in the Officers-in-Training box or any remaining pensioner. If still none are available, the {frontmatter._['role.govt.governor.full']} assumes the role.
