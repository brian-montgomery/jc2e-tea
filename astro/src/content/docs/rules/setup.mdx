When setting up this scenario, follow the usual rules for setup, but use the following
instead of step 10:

10. Place the scenario board next to the main board.
    You may place this on the left of the main board to better represent geography,
    or to the right of the main board to better incorporate game flow.
11. Shuffle the Events in America deck and place them near the Indian Event tiles.
12. Place the Native American local alliance tiles on the *Army of North America*
    space.
13. Place a marker on the "S" space on the {frontmatter._["tea.track"]} track
    and on the highest space on the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} track.
14. Place the additional colored six-sided dice next to the Scenario board.
15. Place the additional office, smuggler and {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]} cards nearby.
16. Place the {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} where they are accessible.
17. Place each speculation token in the matching region in America (£ side face **down**).
18. Use the Setup and Scenario Cards.

## Scenario Card

*  Place the ___Tea Regulation___, ___Peace Accord___, and ___Molasses Act___ near the scenario
   board as you would other passed laws.
*  Place one Company Ship in the Superintendent of China's box.
*  Set the Prime Minister Wheel to the "Social Tax plus Window Tax" space halfway in between the
   1710 and 1758 starting spaces.
