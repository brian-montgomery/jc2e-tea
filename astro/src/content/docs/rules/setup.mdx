When setting up this scenario, follow the usual rules for setup, but use the following
instead of step 10:

10. Place the scenario board next to the main board.
    _Place this on the left of the main board to better represent geography,
    or to the right of the main board to better incorporate game flow_.
11. Shuffle the Events in America deck and place them near the Indian Event tiles.
12. Place the Native American local alliance tiles on the *Army of North America*
   space.
13. Place the colored six-sided dice next to the Scenario board.
14. Place the additional office, smuggler, {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]},  cards nearby.
15. Place the {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} where they are accessible.
16. Place each speculation token in the matching region in America (£ side face **down**).
17. Use the Setup and Scenario Cards.

## Scenario Card

*  Place the ___Tea Regulation___, ___Peace Accord___, and ___Molasses Act___ near the scenario
   board as you would other passed laws.
*  Place one Company Ship in the Superintendent of China's box.
*  Set the Prime Minister Wheel to the "Social Tax and Window Tax" space halfway in between the
   1710 and 1758 starting spaces.

## The Boards

:::note[Main board setup]

Use the following map and instructions to setup India:

![The initial map for the 1733 scenario](../../../assets/images/india-setup.png)

* Closed Orders
  * All orders in ___Punjab___ and ___Hyderabad___ are closed.
  * The two southernmost orders in ___Delhi___ are closed (£3 and £5).
  * The southernmost orders in ___Maratha___ (£2), ___Madras___ (£4), and ___Mysore___ (£6) are closed.
* Region Strength
  * ___Maratha___ has a strength of 2.
  * ___Punjab___ has a strength of 0.
  * All other regions have a strength of 1.
* Empires
  * One empire is located in the north, with ___Delhi___ dominating ___Punjab___.
  * There is one empire in the south, with ___Madras___ serving ___Hyderabad___.
* The elephant is pointing from ___Madras___ into ___Hyderabad___.
:::

:::note[Scenario board setup]

Use the following map and instructions to setup America:

* Closed Orders
  * The two western £2 orders in the ___Delawarean___ and the ___Southern Provinces___ are closed.
  * The westernmost £3 order in the ___Chesapeake___ is closed.
  * The £3 orders in ___Canada___, ___Florida___, the ___Mississippi Reserve___, and the ___Ohio Country___ are closed.
* Region Strength
  * ___Canada___ and __Florida__ have a strength of 2.
  * ___Mississippi Reserve___ and the ___Ohio Country___ has a strength of 1.
* Allied Territory
  * ___Canada___ and ___Ohio Country___ should be allied together.
:::
