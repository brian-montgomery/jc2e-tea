This is an overview of the new concepts and differences introduced in this scenario.

## {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]}

The British Government placed its Atlantic colonies under the Southern Department (and later the Colonial Office). In {frontmatter._["game.title"]}, this department acts like a Presidency that maintains its own Army, offices, tax collectors and --potentially later in the game-- fleet of ships. These resources are stored in their marked boxes or in the Atlantic Ocean.

When hiring, the Company does not hire family members in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} and vice-versa.

## {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} and Debt

Whenever debt is removed from Company, {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} or a Firm, raise the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by that amount. Whenever the Company, {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} or a Firm takes debt, lower the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by that amount. If the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} is at 0 and cannot be lowered, only emergency loans may be taken.

The red cubes are used to track debt in for the {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} and firms.

## America

The firms trade in America, not in India. During the China phase, trading and military operations happen in America.

<div class="indent">
   ### Colonized Regions

   Any region in America that is controlled by the British Government is a **colonized region**. This term is used to differentiate them from the Company **controlled regions** in India.

   ### Atlantic Sea Zone

   The east coast of America is treated as one large sea zone. However, each storm is localized around each individual port. All firm ships are associated with the port matching their chosen strategy, unless the "Hobnob" strategy is chosen.
</div>

## Tea

The {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} are acquired during Trade in China. Afterwards, the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} conducts a Tea Auction, which provides tea to the firms trading in America. Any leftover is stored in the Company warehouses.

Trading tea allows the firms to get more value out of a trade order in a colonized region, at the risk of rolling less die per unrest.
