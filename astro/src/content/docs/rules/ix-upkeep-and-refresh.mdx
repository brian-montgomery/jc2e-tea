When preparing the board for the next turn, do the following:
*  All {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} are stored in the Company's warehouses. Move them to the
   lower half of the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}' (or {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}'s) treasury.
*  Any exhausted officers and military units in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.military.full"]}'s become active.
   Any active local alliances are exhausted.
*  Return collectors to the {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]}'s treasury.
