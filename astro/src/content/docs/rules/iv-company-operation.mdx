## Superintendent of Trade in China

In this scenario, the Superintendent of China is open at the start.

<div class="indent">
### Trade in China: Silver

When trading in China, if the company has no export icons in Company-controlled regions, the Superintendent may trade with silver. When trading with silver, a successful trade earns (a) £2 for the Company Balance, and (b) for the rest of the turn, act as if the company has one company-controlled export icon. _This export icon is used for the Superintendent's bonus and acquiring Tea_.
</div>

## {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}

The {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} is responsible for managing Tea and their turn takes place during the Tea phase, after China.

Unlike other treasuries, the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}' treasury only contains
{frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]}. Like a Commander's box, the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}'s
treasury has a top and bottom half. The top half contains active {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]}
({frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} that were acquired this turn and are added to the Company's Expenses).
The bottom half holds inactive {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]}, stored in the Company's warehouses.

During the Tea phase, take the following actions:

<div class="indent">

   ### Acquire Tea

   If Trade in China was successful, the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} acquires Tea to trade.
   Determine the number of export icons in company-controlled regions (1 if [silver](#trade-in-china-silver) was used to trade) and multiply that by the current value on the {frontmatter._["tea.track"]}, [rounding to the closest number](#tea-conversion). Place that resulting amount of {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} in the top half of your treasury.

   ### Auction

   You may exchange {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} from your treasury (from the top, the bottom or both) to the firms, up to their number of ships. A firm must pay **a minimum** of £1 for each {frontmatter._["tea.token.singular"]} bought. All funds exchanged from the firm must be paid from the Trade Bid space to the Company Balance. _Any other additional terms may be negotiated._

   Each firm may have a number of {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} up to their amount of ships.
   _A firm cannot procure more {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} than it can hold_.

## {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}

The {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]} is created by passing the
[**Tea Regulation**](/jc2e-tea/rules/viii-parliament-meets/#the-vote-to-regulate-tea) act.
When passed, the player holding the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} card should replace
it with the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]} card and place the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}
overlay on the ribbon. Any fatigue on the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} office card
should be moved to the new office card. Return the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} card to the box.

The {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]} is hired like the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} by the Chairman and
is subject to attrition like any other office. The {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]}’s treasury is now used by the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}.

<div class="indent">
### Acquire Tea

If Trade in China was successful, the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} acquires Tea to trade.
Determine the number of export icons in company-controlled regions (1 if [silver](#trade-in-china-silver) was used to trade) and multiply that by the current value on the {frontmatter._["tea.track"]}, [rounding to the closest number](#tea-conversion). Place that resulting amount of {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} in the top half of your treasury.

### Trade Tea

When the {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]} allows Trade in America, the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]} determines their dice pool with the following steps:

1. Declare the **colonized regions in America** in which you wish to trade. You cannot include regions that are foreign settled, regions that you could not trade in because you do not have enough ships or because you could not fill an order because a connecting order is closed. **Consider the three ports in America to be connected.**

2. Make a check. Add one die per {frontmatter._["tea.token.singular"]} in your treasury that you wish to trade. You may use {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} in either half of your treasury. Subtract one die for each unrest cube in each region that you declared in step 1 from your total dice.

**If successful**, follow the firms rule for initiative. When it's your turn to place writers, place one filled order token **for each ship** in the declared regions, starting with a port on one of those regions (just as you would in India).
   *  For each _order filled_, increase the Company Balance by the amount listed on the order.
   *  For each _region you traded in_, add £1 to the Colonial Treasury.

:::bonus
Take £1 from the bank for each order you filled.
:::

Afterwards, discard all {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} used to roll die.
