## Company
*  Add active {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} to the Company expenses.
*  Decrease the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each emergency loan taken.

## Firms
*  When calculating expenses, count each debt (red cube) as a share.
*  Firms may take emergency investment or emergency loans. When taking an emergency loan:
   *  Place a red cube in the firm's Shares box for each loan taken. Do not count these as
      additional expenses for this round.
   *  Decrease the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} for each loan taken.
*  When paying dividends, count each red cube as a share. Each must be paid per dividend. _The bank will not elect to not be paid._

## {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]}
*  Calculate expenses for {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} (Military and Debt), paying them from its treasury.
   *  The {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor"]} may take the first debt as normal, not as an emergency loan.
   *  Any additional debt is taken as emergency loans.
