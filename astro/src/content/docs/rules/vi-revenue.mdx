During the Revenue phase, the Company will pay expenses, check expectations,
and then pay dividends. The firms will pay expenses and pay dividends. The
{frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} will pay expenses. If unable to pay
expenses, all of them must take emergency loans.

Below is the list of changes to this phase.

## Expenses

One at a time, have each entity pay its expenses.

<div class="indent">
### Company Expenses
Lower the Company Balance marker by £1 for each debt as indicated by
the Company Debt track, each regiment and officer in an Army (do not include
Commanders), each ship in the three sea zones and **each active
{frontmatter._["tea.token.singular"]}** to the Company expenses.

*  **Emergency Loans.**
   For each emergency loan taken, lower the amount of {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by 1.

### Department Expenses

The {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]} must pay for the following expenses:
*  Pay £1 for each officer and military unit in the Army.
*  Pay £1 for each red debt cube in the Colonial Treasury.

When the department cannot pay all of its expenses, it must receive emergency funding.

*  __Emergency Funding.__
   If the Department cannot pay its expenses, emergency funding must be taken to
   cover those expenses. For each emergency loan taken, add one red debt cube and
   add £5 to the Colonial Treasury. Do this until all expenses have been met.
   Additional red debt cubes do not incur expenses during this game turn.

   For each emergency funding received, lower the amount of {frontmatter._["credit.track"]}
   by 1.

*  __Public Embarrassment.__
   If more than one emergency funding is required this turn (more than two if
   [War is Declared](/jc2e-tea/rules/concepts/#declaration-of-war)), the
   {frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]} is immediately fired. Return its
   family marker to your supply and place its office card in the Vacant Offices box.

### Firm Expenses

Each firm must pay for the following expenses:
*  Pay £1 for each ship on the firm's charter.
*  Pay £1 for each share **and bank loan** in the firm's Share box or pay £ equal to the firm value,
whichever is higher.

A firm that cannot pay expenses must receive an emergency investment, merger **or loan** or it will be dissolved.
*  **Emergency Loans.**
   A firm facing dissolution may receive an emergency loan from the bank and use that money
   to cover expenses just like an emergency investment. Any cash remaining after expenses are
   paid is lost. Place a red debt cube in the firms's Shares box. _Even if a firm is at the ten
   shares limit, a debt cube is still added to the Shares box._

   Finally, lower the firm's value and the amount of {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by 1
   per emergency loan received.
</div>


## Dividends

The Company may pay dividends, just like the other scenarios.

### Firm Dividends

The manager may choose to pay out a number of dividends using cash
in the firm's India treasury. For each dividend, £1 for each share is paid
to the player owning that share, __£1 is given to the bank for each debt,__
and £1 is given to the player who is the manager of the firm. All cash from
dividends is only taken from the India treasury.

*  When paying dividends, each red debt cube must be paid per dividend.
   _The bank will never elect to not be paid._
