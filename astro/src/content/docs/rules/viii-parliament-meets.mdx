In _{frontmatter._["game.title"]}_, Parliament has a bit more responsibility
and reach. The following are additions to this phase:

## Acts from the {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]}

While the Prime Minister is selecting a law to put before Parliament, the
{frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs.full"]} is responsible for choosing a
recommendation from the Board of Trade. They do this by drawing and revealing
two Act cards from the {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]} deck. The
{frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]} will choose one Act to be enacted
if the selected law passes. The other act will be enacted if the selected
law fails.

After the Prime Minister reads the law and marks its starting support
(printed on bottom right of law), the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]}
reads aloud the chosen Trade Act to be enacted. The Prime Minister and
{frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]} should allow some time for debate
before proceeding to voting.

During the vote, the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]} is not restricted
in voting either way.

*  __Vacant {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]}.__
   If the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]} is vacant, before drawing
   any law cards, the Prime Minister must appoint a _First Lord of Trade_
   for the turn. The Prime Minister is free to appoint any player, other
   than themselves and the player who was the
   {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs.full"]} most recently. The _First Lord of Trade_
   performs all of the same actions the {frontmatter._["role.govt.tariffs"]}
   would during Parliament Meets.

## Resolving the Vote: Additions

Based on how the vote resolves, one of the Trade Acts will be enacted.
*  Apply any immediate effects of the enacted Trade Act and place it next to the Scenario Board.
   If the newly enacted Trade Act is a _Colonial Duty_, place it on top of the previous _Colonial Duty_.

Discard the other Trade Act that was not enacted.

## The Vote to Regulate Tea

In this scenario, the Prime Minister may initiate a special session of Parliament at the start of the turn before the London Season phase. To do this, the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} must be less than either the Company Debt or the amount of stored {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]}. If the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} is less than both the Company Debt and the amount of stored {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]}, the Prime Minister **must** initiate a special session to vote on Tea Regulation.

When voting, the starting support should start with the amount of stored {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]} minus the amount of {frontmatter._["credit.track"]}.

:::note[Example of the starting support]
If the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} is 5 and the Company has 8 stored {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]},
`8 - 5 = 3` and support for the vote would start at +3. Similarly, if the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} is 7 and the Company has 6 stored {frontmatter._["tea.token.plural"]}, `6 - 7 = -1`, and support for the vote would start at -1.
:::

If the vote passes, increase the {frontmatter._["tea.track"]} by one space. Place the Standing marker on the “S” space on the track and place 3 Company ships in the Atlantic. The Prime Minister gains a passed law token. Change the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea"]} to the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}, per the rules in the {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]} section. Refer to the Tea Regulation rules for additional changes.

If the law fails, increase the value of Company shares by placing (or flipping) the VP overlay in the Court. Shares cannot be worth more than +/- 3 victory points. __Unlike other proposed laws, this law is not associated with a policy or an act from the {frontmatter._["deck.laws.singular"]} and the Prime Minister does not change hands if it fails nor will this law be discarded. The Prime Minister may also vote against the law.__
