## Family Action

There are a couple of changes with some of the family actions.

<div class="indent">
   <div class="sl-flex" style="margin-top: 0.25em; margin-right: 0.25em; column-gap: 1.5em;">
      <div>
      ### Enlist Writer

      When placing writers, players may place a piece in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.customs.full"]}'s
      treasury (on the scenario board) instead of a presidency. This piece is a **collector**.
      _When counting for vacant offices,
      do not include the offices in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.office"]}._
      </div>

<div class="indent">

### Enlist Officer

When placing officers, players may place a piece in the {frontmatter._["role.govt.military.full"]}'s box
(on the scenario board), instead of the Officers-in-Training box.

**If [War is Declared](/jc2e-tea/rules/vii-events/#war-declared-), you may place
an additional officer once this turn.** _This might allow you to place two or three
officers in a turn depending on the location of your opportunity marker._

</div>

## New Company Shares

For each space the Company Debt is lowered (as shares are added), raise the {frontmatter._["credit.track"]} by 1.
