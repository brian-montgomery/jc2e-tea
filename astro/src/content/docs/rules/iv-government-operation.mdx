Each President generates revenue for the Company with the Trade action and
decides the order in which they, their Commander, and their Governor(s) act.
Commanders and Governors are not required to take any actions but they must

Presidency’s operations so long as it does not interrupt another position.

## {frontmatter._["role.company.tea2"]}

The Consignor of Tea is created when the Tea Act is passed. The player holding the Director of Warehouses card should replace it with the Consignor of Tea card and place the Consignor of Tea overlay on the position to remind all players. Any fatigue on the Director of Warehouses office card should be moved to the new office card.

Unlike a President’s treasury, the Consigner's treasury only contains tea chests. Ships can be sent to the Atlantic sea zone (next to America) by the Manager of Shipping or the Director of Trade as if it were a sea zone in India. These ships count as if they were in the ?????? sea zone for storms, expenses, events, and laws.

When trading, the Consigner determines their dice pool differently:

1. Declare the regions in which you wish to trade. You cannot include regions that you could not trade in because you do not have enough ships or because you could not fill an order because a connecting order is closed. *Consider the three ports in America to be connected.*

2. Make a check.
   - Add one die per tea chest in your treasury. You may move up any number of exhausted tea chests (the tokens in your warehouse).
      :::tip[Alternative]{icon="star"}
      - Add one die for each tea chest you spend from the warehouse and one die for each set of (a) tea chest you spend from your treasury, along with (b) £1 from the Company Balance.
      :::
   - Subtract one for each unrest cube in each region that you declared in step 1 from your total dice.

If successful, follow the firms rule for initiative. When it's your turn to place writers, place one writer **for each ship** in the declared regions, starting with a port on one of those regions (just as you would in India). For each order filled, increase the Company Balance by the amount listed on the order.

Afterward, subtract £1 from each tea chest spent from your treasury and discard all tea chests used to roll die.

Take £1 from the bank for each order you filled.

When the Tea Act is repealed, the player holding the Consignor of Tea should switch back to the Director of Warehouses, replacing the office card and removing the overlay. Any fatigue should also be transfered to the new office card.
