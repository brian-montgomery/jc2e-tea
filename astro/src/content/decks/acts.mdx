This is the deck representing the recommendations from the Board of Trade.
The **{frontmatter._["role.govt.governor.full"]}** (or the First Lord of Trade)
will draw two cards during the **Parliament Meets** phase and decide which should be
applied when the vote passes and which should be applied if the vote fails.

Two cards, _Tea Regulation_ and _Peace Accord_, have custom backs.
Both of these and the _Molasses Act_ should not be shuffled in with the rest of
the deck, as play starts with all three of them face-up.
