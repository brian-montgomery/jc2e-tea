Once per turn, when successfully
filling an order, a firm may place
a family marker facing down underneath
the filled order token or collector.
Lower the amount received by the order's
previously-filled value.

**Do not** remove this facedown marker
during upkeep.

In a later turn, when that firm successfully
trades on that order, take the marker back
to receive the full value of that order
(in addition the current trade value).
